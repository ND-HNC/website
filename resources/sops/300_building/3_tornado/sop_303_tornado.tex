\section{Policies \& Procedures}

Procedures for building responses to tornado safety and severe weather emergencies are detailed.


% notice
\subsection{Notification of Severe Weather or Tornado}
\label{ssec:tornado-notif}

Persons should maintain awareness of severe weather events or tornados in the area. Increase your vigilance when a Tornado Watch is in effect. Notifications can be received via:
\begin{enumerate}
	\item Weather service alert,
	\item Local news,
	\item Notre Dame emergency warning system (text, call, email).
\end{enumerate}

% notice
\subsection{Location of Tornado Shelters}
\label{ssec:tornado-shelter}

A tornado warning means ``take action'' because a tornado has been sighted or indicated by weather radar. When a tornado warning is announced all persons should seek immediate shelter in the tornado shelter areas and move away from all windows.

Tornado Shelter Areas:
\begin{enumerate}
	\item ND-HNC Reception area,
	\item South stairwell.
\end{enumerate}

See Fig. \ref{fig:vfpc-floor0}.
