% notice
\subsection{Notification of Tornado or Severe Weather}
\label{ssec:tornado-notif}
Maintain awareness of severe weather events or tornados in the area and increase your vigilance when a Tornado Watch is in effect. Notifications can be received via:

\begin{enumerate}
	\item Weather service alert,
	\item Local news,
	\item Notre Dame emergency warning system (text, call, email).
\end{enumerate}

% notice
\subsection{Location of Tornado Shelters}
\label{ssec:tornado-shelter}
When a Tornado Warning is announced all persons should seek immediate shelter in the tornado shelter areas:

\begin{enumerate}
	\item ND-HNC Reception area (Fig \ref{fig:vfpc-floor0}, T1),
	\item South stairwell (Fig \ref{fig:vfpc-floor0}, T2).
\end{enumerate}
